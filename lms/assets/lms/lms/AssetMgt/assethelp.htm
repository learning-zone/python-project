RETURN OF ASSETS
This is used to return the materials / assets which are not working / damaged. The damaged items /materials will be first sent to the central stores from departments and then the items / materials are sent back to the vendor for repair / replacement. Once the items /materials are repaired will be received to the Central Stores & then the Central Stores will issue the items to the departments & then the department incharge will receive the items , inspect the items & send the inspection report to the Central Stores incharge.
Return Assets to Central Stores
  1. Steps involved in returning assets to central stores.
    1. Select Department
    2. Click on the checkbox on which the items are not working
    3. Enter the Quantity
    4. Click on Save Details button to send the damaged materials to Central Stores.
TOP
Return Assets to Vendors
  1. Steps involved in returning assets to Vendors
    1. Select Vendor
    2. List of Items to be returned to the Vendor will come.
    3. Click on the checkbox on which the items are to be returned.
    4. Click on Generate Gate Pass button to send the damaged materials to Vendor.
TOP
Receive Return Assets to Central Stores
  1. Steps involved in receiving returned assets to Central Stores
    1. Select Vendor
    2. List of Items come from the Vendor will be displayed.
    3. Click on the checkbox on which the items are to be received.
    4. Enter the Quantity that are received.
    5. Click on Receive Returned Materials button to receive the repaired / replaced materials from the Vendor.
TOP
Issue returned Assets To Departments
  1. Steps involved in Issuing returned assets to Departments
    1. Select Department
    2. List of Items to be returned to the Department will be displayed.
    3. Click on the checkbox on which the items are to be issued.
    4. Enter the Quantity that are to be issued.
    5. Click on Issue Returned Materials button to send the repaired / replaced materials to the Departments.
TOP
Receive returned Assets To Departments
  1. Steps involved in receiving returned assets to Departments
    1. Select Department
    2. List of Items to be received to the Department will be displayed.
    3. Inspect the items.
    4. Click on the checkbox on which the items are inspected & working properly.
    5. Enter the Quantity that are working properly.
    6. Click on Receive Returned Materials To Departments button to receive the repaired / replaced materials to the Departments.
The inspection report will be sent to Incharge of Central Stores, the Central Stores incharge will inturn generate the Asset Numbers for the returned materials.
TOP
