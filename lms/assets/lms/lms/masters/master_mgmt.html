Manage School Details














To Add/Modify School Details

  1. Enter the School Name,School Code,address,City,State,Pin Code,Phone,Fax,E-mail and TIN No in the text boxes at the bottom of the page under the headings Manage School Details .
  2. Click the Button Add.
  3. Ensure that the Details added are displayed in the list of the text boxes above.

 













Modify

  1. The School Details provided can be changed OR Modified here.
  2. Edit the text in the textfields .
  3. Click the Button Modify. (The data is updated).

Manage School Division

To Add School Division Details into the List:

  1. Add School Division Name and Abbreviation .
  2. Type the text ('School Division/School Division abbr') in the text box .
  3. Click the button Add.
  4. Ensure that the School Division/School Division abbr' name is displayed correctly in the list above.

To Modify a School Division/School Division abbr in a list.

  1. Identify the School Division/School Division abbr name to be Modified.
  2. Click the check box of the respective School Division/School Division abbr in the select menu.
  3. Edit the School Division/School Division abbr in the text box and click button Modify to update the details.
  4. Ensure that the School Division/School Division abbr is displayed correctly in the list or been modified.

Manage Nationality

To add the intake to the Semesters/Year of a course.

  1. Select the Course Name and click the button Get Intake.
  2. Select the Semester/Year from the drop down menu below and type the intake in the text box.
  3. Click the button Add.
  4. Ensure that it is displayed properly in the list above.

    To modify/delete the intake of the Semester/Year of a course:

  5. Repeat the Step 1
  6. Check the check box of the respective Year/Semester and change the intake in the text box and click the button modify to update the data.
  7. Click delete to delete the record.
