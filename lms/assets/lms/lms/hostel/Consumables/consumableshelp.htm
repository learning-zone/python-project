MASTER ENTRIES

ITEM MASTER

  1. Steps to Enter Item Master
    1. Enter Item Name
    2. Enter Units ( Measuring Units)
    3. Click on Add Item button.
  2. Steps to Modify Item Master Details
    1. Click on the Checkbox for which modification is to be done.
    2. Modify Item Name / Units
    3. Click on Modify button
TOP
PURCHASE MASTER
  1. Steps to Enter Purchase Master Details
    1. Enter Purchase Date
    2. Select Supplier
    3. Select Item , Enter Quantity,Unitprice and click add to add purchased items .
    4. Click on Save Purchase Details to add the Purchased Items.
TOP
ISSUE MASTER
  1. Steps to Enter Issue Master Details
    1. Select Account Head
    2. Enter Issue Date
    3. Select Department
    4. Select the Staff for which the Items are to be issued.
    5. Select Item, enter quantity and click add items to add.
    6. Select the Staff who is issuing items.
    7. Click on Save Issue Details to Issue the Items to the staff.
TOP
